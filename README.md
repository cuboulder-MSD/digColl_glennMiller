# Glenn Miller Digital Collection

## Description
Alton "Glenn" Miller was one of the most well known big band leaders of the 1930s-1940s. He attended the University of Colorado between 1923-1924 before moving on to pursue his musical career. This collection contains material from the Glenn Miller Collections relating to the personal life and professional career of Glenn Miller. Materials include original correspondence, telegrams, and film reels from Glenn's time conducting his civilian orchestra from 1939-1942. Photographs document Glenn and his wife Helen Burger's childhoods, with images of relatives, band members, and close family. Manuscripts written by Glenn's friends and band members document their time playing in Glenn's orchestra and serving with him during WWII in Glenn's Army Air Force Band. Family photo albums and scrapbooks by fans provide comprehensive documentation of the life and times of Glenn Miller.
